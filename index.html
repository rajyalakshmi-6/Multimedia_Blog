1.🎥Dear Comrade (2019) - A Powerful Love Story with Anger and Ideals

Watch the Video Here

Language: Telugu
Genre: Romantic Drama
Director: Bharat Kamma
Cast:Vijay Deverakonda, Rashmika Mandanna
About the Movie: Dear Comrade is an intense romantic drama that explores love, anger, and emotional healing. The film follows Bobby, a student leader with severe anger issues, and Lilly, a state-level cricketer who believes in calmness and self-control. Their love story unfolds through emotional highs and painful conflicts, making the film deeply relatable and raw.

2.🎥🔥 KGF: Chapter 1 (2018) > Rise of a Monster

🎬 About the Movie KGF: Chapter 1 tells the gripping story of Rocky, a man born into poverty who rises through violence, ambition, and determination to become the most feared name in the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF). Set between the 1950s–1980s, the film blends raw action with a powerful mother–son emotion, making it one of the most iconic Indian films ever made.The narrative style is unique, intense, and larger-than-life, turning Rocky into a modern cinematic legend.
